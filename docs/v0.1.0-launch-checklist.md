## What we deliberately skipped for v0.1.0

- **Telemetry / analytics inside the app.** Zero. Mental-health entries
  are too sensitive for the standard-tier privacy story that dev tools
  can get away with. The only network call is license validation.
- **Cloud sync.** By design — see [AGENTS.md § Why these choices](AGENTS.md).
  The encrypted backup file is the migration path. Sync is a v0.4
  Pro-Plus consideration only.
- **Push notifications.** Deliberate — a mental-health tool that
  pushes at the wrong moment does harm. Users open Ledger when they
  choose to.
- **AI advice.** By design and messaged in the tagline. The digest
  ranks and surfaces user-authored reframes; it never generates
  therapeutic content.
- **Social / streak features.** Same. Streaks reward showing up; therapy
  material rewards showing up *at the right times*. Different goal.
- **A "share your entry" button.** Users can print or export encrypted
  backups. Direct-share is a v0.3 therapist-mode feature with careful
  UX around consent.

## Kill criteria (see also [AGENTS.md § Kill criteria](AGENTS.md))

At day 60:
- <30 unique PWA installs on the deployed origin → the launch channel
  isn't reaching the audience. Reconsider channels before iterating on
  the product.
- <3 paying customers in 90 days → the $79 anchor isn't clearing the
  "I could use Notion" bar. Consider bundle-with-Pattern-Library or
  Pro-Plus tier ahead of schedule.
